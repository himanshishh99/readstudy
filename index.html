Before You Begin

How This Works

You will read 6 short passages (about 200 words each) and answer a few questions after each one. The whole thing takes about 10 to 15 minutes.

  • 1We will ask permission to use your webcam to track your eye movements while you read. No video is recorded — only gaze coordinates.
  • 2You will do a quick calibration — just click 9 dots on screen. Takes about 1 minute.
  • 3Read each passage at your natural pace. There is no time limit. Click Done Reading when you finish.
  • 4Answer 5 short questions about each passage. Be honest — there are no right or wrong answers beyond what the text says.
  • 5Sit in a well-lit room, keep your head relatively still, and make sure your face is visible to the webcam.

Your responses are completely anonymous and will only be used for research purposes. You can stop at any time.
